Limitations of HealthWise

While HealthWise is a powerful tool for symptom-based disease diagnosis, it does have some limitations:

  • Preprocessed Datasets: The system relies on preprocessed datasets. Symptoms not present in the dataset will result in an error or no prediction.
  • Comma-Separated Symptoms: Symptoms must be entered in a comma-separated format with underscores (e.g., skin_rash, chills) rather than spaces.
  • Error Handling: If the input symptoms do not match the dataset or are formatted incorrectly, the application may not function as expected.

I am continually working to improve HealthWise and expand its capabilities. Your feedback and suggestions are always welcome!
